Starting with templates

25 Oct 2021 - Matthias Mair

A good starting-point for developing your own plugin is taking a look at the documentation.

You could also use one of the current plugins as a sample. Search for something that is similar to the wanted functionality and use it as a template.

Be aware of the licenses of plugins and the restrictions that come with them.

The ecosystem is just getting started so if something does not make sense for you or could be done easier: We welcome feedback and invite you to submit a feature request to InvenTree. If you have remarks about this platform: we have discussions enabled.
